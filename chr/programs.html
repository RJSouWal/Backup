Swim Lessons

Ages 6mo-3yrs w/parent Parent & Baby Participation Description:
Designed to be an infant's first introduction to water adjustment with an emphasis on parent participation / education as well as safety skills and fun. One child per adult.
Ages 3-5yrs Star Tots - Level 1
Star Tots - Level 2
Star Tots - Level 3		 Description:
For preschool children who are ready to attend class on their own. Focus on swim readiness skills and safety.
Ages 5+ Swim School-Level 1
Swim School-Level 2
Swim School-Level 3 		Description:
For children over 5 yrs old beginning with little or no formal swim instruction. Class emphasis on breath control, submerging, front & back floats to intro to treading and freestyle and backstroke.
Ages 5+ Stroke School-L 1
Stroke School-L 2
Stroke School-L 3 		Description:
For children over 5 yrs old with a good water skills foundation. Students will go from freestyle and backstroke review to butterfly, followed by breaststroke with skills for swim team.
Ages 16+ Adult -Beginners
Adult - Stroke School 		Description:
For adults over 16 yrs old at any stage from beginning water adjustment to stroke refinement.
